About Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful, landlocked country filled with natural wonders. It is home to Mount Everest (Sagarmatha), the highest peak in the world, and Lumbini, the sacred birthplace of Gautama Buddha. The country is globally renowned for its rich culture, breathtaking scenery, meditation, ancient heritage, and extreme adventure tourism.

📍 Places to Visit

Kathmandu

The capital city of Nepal, famous for its historic Durbar Squares, and sacred shrines like Pashupatinath and Swayambhunath.

Pokhara

A scenic lakeside city offering breathtaking views of the Annapurna range and Mount Machhapuchhre. It serves as the main gateway for trekking.

Muktinath

A sacred and popular pilgrimage site located in the Mustang district, highly revered by both Hindus and Buddhists alike.

🥾 Trekking Destinations

Everest Base Camp (EBC)

One of the most famous and adventurous trekking routes in the world, leading you directly to the base of the world's highest peak.

Annapurna Circuit

An incredible trek crossing the Thorong La Pass (5,416m), packed with diverse cultural experiences and stunning alpine scenery.

Langtang Valley

Located close to Kathmandu, this trek features beautiful alpine valleys, glaciers, and traditional Tamang culture.
