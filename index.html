🇳🇵 About Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful landlocked country in South Asia. It is famous for Mount Everest, the highest mountain in the world, Lumbini (the birthplace of Gautama Buddha), ancient temples, rich culture and adventure tourism.

📍 Places To Visit

📍 Kathmandu

Capital city of Nepal famous for temples, Durbar Squares and historical places.

📍 Pokhara

Beautiful lakeside city with Annapurna mountain views.

📍 Muktinath

A holy pilgrimage site for Hindus and Buddhists.

🥾 Trekking Destinations

🏔️ Everest Base Camp

One of the world's most famous trekking routes.

🏔️ Annapurna Circuit

A beautiful trek with mountains and cultural experiences.

🏔️ Langtang Valley

A peaceful valley near Kathmandu with Tamang culture.
